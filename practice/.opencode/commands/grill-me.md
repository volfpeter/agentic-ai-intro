Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding.

Try to identify unknowns, assumptions, edge-cases and help me understand those.

Question how the plan fits into the project.

If applicable, walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
